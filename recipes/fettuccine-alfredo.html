Fettuccine Alfredo

Description:

Fettuccine pasta with a rich and creamy Alfredo sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta - Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter - Two Sticks
  • Cream - Cup of Heavy Cream
  • Seasonings - Salt, Pepper, and Garlic Salt
  • Cheese - Romano and Parmesan Cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook the Pasta
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce
